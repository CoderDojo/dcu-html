Coder Dojo @ DCU

Welcome to the first Coderdojo session in DCU. We have very few rules, but our main one is to be cool. We will only teach areas that are cool such as web site, android, iPhone and game development. If there is something cool out there we have not seen let us know. This course will start with basics of HTML and then bring you on an adventure to build what ever you imagination decides on the internet. Some of the most popular pages on the internet have:

  • Games
  • Videos
  • Blogs
  • Social Media
  • Cool images
