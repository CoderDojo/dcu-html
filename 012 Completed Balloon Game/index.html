Rules

  • Blow up the balloon by clicking on the logo
  • You never know when its going to blow
  • The balloon has a max blows of 11
  • You get 10 points for every successful blow
  • If you think the balloon is at its max, hit the tie button
  • If you quit too early you loose a life
  • If you blow too many times you loose a life
  • If you guess right you get extra 100 points
  • If you guess right your next round blows increase by 10 points
  • Challenge yourself and see what your highest score is
  • If you are happy with your highest score hit "Happy" to end the game
